Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we protect your data in Rearriva VR.

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

Rearriva VR ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you play Rearriva VR on supported VR platforms.

1. Information We Collect

  • Platform-provided data (e.g., Oculus/Meta user ID, display name)
  • Gameplay data (playtime, in-game actions, player behavior)
  • Crash reports and bug logs to improve stability

We do not collect personal identifiers such as your real name, physical address, or payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Improve gameplay experience and app performance
  • Detect and prevent cheating or abuse
  • Support moderation and enforce community guidelines
  • Analyze crashes and collect feedback for development

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your information.

We may share limited data with trusted third-party services (analytics or platform moderation tools) only as necessary for app functionality, safety, or compliance.

4. Children's Privacy

Rearriva VR is intended for users aged 13 and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware of such data collected without parental consent, it will be promptly deleted.

5. Your Rights

  • Access personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection where applicable

To exercise these rights, contact us at: somethinggames@gmail.com

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app messages or platform updates.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, reach out via email or join our Discord community.
