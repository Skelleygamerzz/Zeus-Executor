FAQ

How do I get in contact with an HR?
Members of High-Rank team (Junior Corporate+) can be contacted through Discord or Roblox individually. However, making a ticket is the best option for communicating with our higher-ranked staff for the best responses to questions or concerns.

How do I make a suggestion?
Join our Discord and make your suggestion in the #suggestions channel! These are voted on by the rest of the server and could potentially be put into place if noticed by an HR+.

Are you hiring developers?
At this time, Vienna's Cafe is not looking to hire any developers. If this changes, check our social media for any announcement or applications that may be posted.
