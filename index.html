Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we protect your data in Rearriva VR.

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Rearriva VR ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you play Rearriva VR on supported VR platforms.

1

Information We Collect

Rearriva VR minimizes personal data collection. We may collect the following types of information:

  • Platform-provided data, such as Oculus/Meta user ID or display name
  • Gameplay data, including playtime, in-game actions, and player behavior
  • Crash reports and bug logs to improve stability and performance

‚úì Privacy First

We do not collect personal identifiers such as your real name, physical address, or payment information.

2

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve gameplay experience and app performance
  • Detect and prevent cheating or abuse
  • Support moderation and enforce community guidelines
  • Analyze crashes and collect feedback for development improvements
3

Data Sharing

We do not sell your information.

We may share limited data with trusted third-party services (such as analytics or platform moderation tools), only as necessary for app functionality, safety, or compliance.

4

Children's Privacy

Rearriva VR is intended for users aged 13 and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that such data has been collected without verified parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

5

Your Rights

All users have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain types of data collection where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: somethinggames@gmail.com

6

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If significant changes are made, we will notify users through in-app messaging or platform updates.
